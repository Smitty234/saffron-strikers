Our Mission

Naše mise

Saffron Strikers Sports Club is a voluntary cricket association founded to unite people through playing, promoting, and developing cricket in the Czech Republic. We champion healthy lifestyles, cultural exchange, community cohesion and social inclusion.

Saffron Strikers Sports Club je dobrovolný kriketový spolek založený za účelem spojování lidí prostřednictvím hraní, propagace a rozvoje kriketu v ČR.

More Than Just Cricket

Víc než jen kriket

Founded December 14, 2025, in Prague. Registered non-profit (z.s.). Competing in CMKS T20 League Division 3 in our debut season 2026.
